Final polish checklist

Container & gutters (lock layout)

Wrap page content in a stable container:

<div className="mx-auto max-w-[1200px] xl:max-w-[1280px] px-6 lg:px-8">


Grid: grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 xl:grid-cols-3 gap-6 lg:gap-7 xl:gap-8.

Card image & hover

Ensure uniform media height and corners:

<div className="aspect-[16/9] overflow-hidden rounded-xl">
  <img className="h-full w-full object-cover transition-transform duration-300 hover:scale-[1.02]"
       loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="640" height="360" />
</div>


Keep shadow subtle: card shadow-sm hover:shadow-md.

Title & meta rhythm

Title: line-clamp-2 text-[20px] font-semibold leading-tight.

Brand/brandline: text-slate-500 text-sm.

Use a space-y-3 stack inside the card to keep consistent vertical rhythm.

Price + status row (baseline-aligned)

<div className="flex items-center gap-3">
  <div className="inline-flex h-9 items-center rounded-full bg-gradient-to-r from-indigo-500 to-violet-500 px-3.5 text-white font-semibold">
    $20.00
  </div>
  <span className="rounded-full bg-emerald-100 px-2.5 py-1 text-xs font-semibold text-emerald-700">
    IN STOCK
  </span>
</div>


Chips / badges (compact & consistent)

Category chips: text-xs font-medium px-2.5 py-1 rounded-full.

Keep icon size 14–16px and gap-1.5.

Use the same badge palette across pages (eco/handmade/customizable).

Actions row (same height everywhere)

<div className="mt-4 grid grid-cols-3 gap-2">
  <Button className="h-10 px-3.5"><IconEdit className="mr-1.5 h-4 w-4"/>Edit</Button>
  <Button variant="secondary" className="h-10 px-3.5"><IconEye className="mr-1.5 h-4 w-4"/>Preview</Button>
  <Button variant="outline" className="h-10 px-3.5" aria-label="Copy link"><IconLink className="mr-1.5 h-4 w-4"/>Copy Link</Button>
</div>


Ensure every icon button has an aria-label and visible focus:ring.

“Show Details” affordance

Make the whole row clickable with a caret:

<button className="mt-3 inline-flex items-center gap-1.5 text-sm font-medium text-slate-600 hover:text-slate-900">
  Show Details <svg className="h-4 w-4" />
</button>


Navbar & brand

Stick with the small wordmark “ShopLynk” (capital S/L); current size/weight looks right.

Active tab underline is good—keep it 2px and avoid heavy gradients in nav.

A11y & perf

Ensure product images have descriptive alt.

First row images can be loading="eager" for LCP; all others lazy.

Check contrast on “IN STOCK/LOW STOCK” pills (target WCAG AA; text ~text-emerald-800 / text-amber-800 on light tints).

Theme tokens (optional but helpful)

:root{
  --brand-50:#eef2ff; --brand-100:#e0e7ff;
  --brand-500:#6366f1; --brand-600:#4f46e5; --brand-700:#4338ca;
}


Use from-[var(--brand-600)] to-[var(--brand-500)] for primary gradient CTAs only; keep secondary/outline neutral to avoid noise.
