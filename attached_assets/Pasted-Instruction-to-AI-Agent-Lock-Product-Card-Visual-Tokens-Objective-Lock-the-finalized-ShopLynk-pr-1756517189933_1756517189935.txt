Instruction to AI Agent — Lock Product Card Visual Tokens

Objective:
Lock the finalized ShopLynk product card design system into global tokens to ensure styling consistency across seller and buyer storefront views.

Scope

Applies to all product cards in seller and buyer storefronts.

Tokens are non-editable unless unlocked by governance.

Tokens override any local hardcoded CSS or component-level styles.

Governance Rules
✅ Allowed:

Dynamic badges can show or hide based on product data.

Text and images remain dynamic.

Cards scale responsively.

⛔ Forbidden:

Do not hardcode any button, pill, or badge color.

Do not locally override shadows, radii, or hover styles.

Do not alter vertical/horizontal spacing per component.

Expected Outcome

Product cards look identical across seller and buyer views.

Tokens control all future UI updates for product cards.

Global system ensures that adding new components won’t break visual hierarchy.
