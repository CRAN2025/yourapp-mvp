Storefront Header + Global Unification

(Governance enforced — scoped for visuals only)

1. Objective

Make the storefront visually identical to the ShopLynk landing page in terms of:

Colors & gradients

Shadows & depth

Typography hierarchy

Button consistency

Unified modern SaaS look

2. Observations from Screenshot

✅ Logo container: Correct square shape, subtle shadows look good.

⚠️ Back button: Slight mismatch vs. landing page CTA gradient and shadow.

⚠️ Pills (category filters): Inactive and active states don’t yet match icy-blue palette.

⚠️ Overall hue: Still leans slightly toward gray; landing page has a cooler, crisp bluish tint.

⚠️ Typography: “Powered by ShopLynk” hierarchy and spacing need alignment.

3. Governance Rules (MUST FOLLOW)

Do not modify business logic, routing, data, or backend integration.

All style tokens must be declared centrally in themeConfig.js or equivalent.

Changes must be responsive and accessibility-compliant.

Perform non-destructive edits — ensure sellers’ storefront content remains intact.
